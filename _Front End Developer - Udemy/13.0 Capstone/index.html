About me

That is a picture of me over on the right there. I grew up in Massachusetts on the beach. I took an interest in leterpress printing and ran small presses, learned typography, platemaking and color separating using a process camera. After being a dot etcher and then a scanner operator for several years, I became a photo retoucher using Photoshop. I stuck there for a good 30+ years. After dabbling in video editing, I took a handful of coding classes and became obsessed with the logic and problem solving aspect of it. With the help of my graphic designer husband, I developed some design skills and naturally moved toward web design.
